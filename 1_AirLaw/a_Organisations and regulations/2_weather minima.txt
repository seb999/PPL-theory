VMC - Visual Meteorological Conditions
IMC - Instruments Meteorological Conditions

VFR - Visual Flight Rules
IFR - Instrument Flight Rules

in VMC you have 2 type of air space
-Controlled 
-uncontrolled

Controlled airspace
-1000 feet vertically
-1500 metres horizontaly to clouds
-5km visibility / 8km if above 10 000 feet

COntrolled airspace with Special VFR
-140 knot max
-Visibility 1.5 km
-free from clouds

Uncontrolle airspace same rules
But exception bellow 3000 feet form sea 
or bellow 1000 feet from the ground
-free from clouds
