ATS (Air traffic Service)
ATS is divided into 3 cathegories
1) ATC (air traffick controle)
2) FIS (Flight information Service)
3) AS (Alerting Service)

Then ATC is divided into 3 cathegories
Airport Control Service
Approch Control Service
Area Control Service

Large Airport have ATIS - Automatic Terminal Information Service

In uncontrolled airpace you can get FIS Flight Information service
They give information in airpace TIZ and TIA AFIS

AFIS is here to help but not to give clearlance
They don;t have a radar so give them your position
