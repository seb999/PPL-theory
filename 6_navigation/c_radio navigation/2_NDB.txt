NDB - Non directional Beacon
Continues transmit carrier waves in all directions 
Invented in 1920 Frequency 255 to 455 Khz (LF MF)
Problem of accurancy
MDB will turn 30degrees over the coast (coast effect)
Mountain effect
Static electricity affect it also
Night effect (radio wave bounce )

2 type of NDB
-locators (range 30NM) identify with 2 letter and frequency
-en route (200NM) identify with 3 letter 

To recieve NDB signal you need a ADF (Automatic direction finder)


NDB -> ADF(receiver)->RBI or RMI (display)

RBI - Relative Bearing Indicator
RMI =Radio magnetic Indicator - the rose show you the direction of the aircraft and you get the TRUE
bearing to the beacon
